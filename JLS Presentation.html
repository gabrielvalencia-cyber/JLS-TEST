Rule-definition workshop · what it looks like
01 · The session

A 90-minute working session we facilitate — not homework for your team.

A small group captures how JLS actually operates, so the tool reflects reality — not a generic template.

2–4Your SMEs — a strong broker or two + a transaction-coordination / ops lead + Unframe PM + Solution engineer
The key de-risk
We don't show up with a blank page. We come in with a draft of the rules — shaped from discovery, your existing process, and the data. Your SMEs react and correct; they don't build from scratch. We do the building afterward.
02 · In the room

What we actually nail down together.

Triggers
What should prompt a broker to reach out — home anniversary, sphere decay at 30 / 60 / 90 days, a life event, a nearby comp — and which to leave off.
Logic & definitions
What counts as "sphere," good vs. bad lead, message tone, exceptions — and (for transactions) how deadlines are calculated: business vs. calendar days, holidays.
Edge cases
The "it depends" situations a top broker knows that aren't written down anywhere.
What comes out
A documented rule set + decisions we turn into the data libraries and logic.
The SMEs validate it · Unframe builds it.
03 · Sample agenda · 90 min · Unframe-facilitated

How the 90 minutes runs.

0–10min
We replay the current process as we understand it — our draft.
10–45min
SMEs correct it — triggers, definitions, tone, exceptions.
45–75min
Walk the edge cases — "how we really do it."
75–90min
Confirm decisions — we own writing it into the platform.
After
We build the libraries → you review async (~1 hr/wk).
